ISPs and Hosts in Wisconsin

Commentary on Internet Service Providers and Hosts in Wisconsin:

  • 5Nines in Madison http://5nines.com/
  • AT&T http://www.att.com/ - Decent DSL service/UVerse
  • NetSonic offers dedicated, colocated, or virtual hosting right in Green Bay, WI. http://www.netsonic.net/
  • Nsight Telservices https://www.nsighttel.com/ - Internet access and web hosting in North East Wisconsin.
  • Spectrum https://www.spectrum.com/ - You can get an Internet-only line.
  • TDS Telecom http://www.tdstelecom.com/ - Primary carrier for certain areas.
